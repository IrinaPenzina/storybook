Ocean love


It talks to me. When I listen to the ocean I can hear it talking to me.

Sometimes I sit in front of it and listen. It tells me my past. It makes me smile sometimes. It makes me cry sometimes.

Sometimes I sit in front of it and listen. It tells me my future. It gives me hope sometimes. It takes my breath away sometimes.

Sometimes I cannot sit in front of the ocean. I dance. It sings me my song. It gives me reason to dream sometimes. It gives me a reason to live sometimes. It gives me a reason to dance sometimes.

The ocean talks to me sometimes...
